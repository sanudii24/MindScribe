🧠 MindScribe

Advanced AI-Powered Mental Health Assistant with Offline Capabilities

Welcome to MindScribe

MindScribe is a cutting-edge, privacy-first mental health companion powered by advanced AI technology. Designed to provide personalized mental health support with complete offline capabilities, MindScribe ensures your data remains yours and yours alone.

Whether you're seeking daily emotional support, mental health assessments, or intelligent conversation with a compassionate AI, MindScribe is here to help you on your wellness journey.

✨ Key Features

🔐 Privacy-First

Complete offline capability with zero data collection. Your conversations stay on your device.

🤖 Advanced AI

Powered by state-of-the-art LLMs with RAG memory architecture for contextual understanding.

🎙️ Voice Support

Natural voice interaction with high-quality TTS and speech recognition capabilities.

💾 Memory Intelligence

Semantic memory system that learns and adapts to your needs over time.

📱 Cross-Platform

Available on Web, Desktop (Windows, macOS, Linux), and mobile platforms.

⚡ High Performance

Optimized inference engine with native CPU support for instant responses.

🏗️ Architecture Highlights

Core Components

  • RAG Pipeline: Retrieval-Augmented Generation for intelligent context-aware responses
  • Memory System: Semantic vector database for long-term conversation memory
  • Inference Engine: Optimized LLM inference with CPU/GPU support
  • Voice Pipeline: End-to-end speech recognition and synthesis
  • Assessment Module: Structured mental health evaluations and tracking
